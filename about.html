About Us

Welcome to Drip and Grind, your neighborhood coffee haven where every sip tells a story. Nestled in the heart of downtown, our coffee shop is a sanctuary for coffee lovers, a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the hum of friendly conversation.

Our Story

Drip and Grind was born out of a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a community hub. Founded by lifelong friends, Alex and Jamie, our journey began with a shared dream of bringing quality coffee and a warm, inviting atmosphere to our community. With backgrounds in coffee roasting and hospitality, we combined our expertise to create a space where people can gather, connect, and enjoy the finest coffee.
